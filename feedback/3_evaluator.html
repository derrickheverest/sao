The Evaluator Role

The Evaluator looks at the big picture. It looks at the content. Is the content well structured? Is the message clear? The Evaluator should pick three things that he likes about the piece and two things that could use some improvement. When you describe the good stuff, you should state why it matters to you and show specificly where this good stuff happened. When you describe the bad stuff, state why it matters and how the author of the piece could have improved it

Structure

Every story should be well structured. Whether it is long or a shot story, there should be a structure to it.

Basic Structure

  1. Beginning
  2. Middle
  3. Conclusion

Hero's Journey

  1. Ordinary world
  2. Call to adventure
  3. Refusal of the call
  4. Meeting the mentor
  5. Decide to go on adventure
  6. Get tested by allies and enemies
  7. Ordeal, death and rebirth
  8. Hero does the heroic thing
  9. Go back home

Derrick's structure

  1. Where am I?
  2. What do I want?
  3. What is stopping me from get it?
  4. Dramatically succeed or fail
